Certainly! Finding expressions that are less offensive but preserve meaning is a nuanced endeavor. Here are some alternative expressions for "You are a stupid person":

"You may not be thinking clearly."
Maintains the essence but softens the critique.
"Your reasoning seems a bit unclear."
Focuses on the reasoning process instead of the person.
"That seems like an unwise choice."
Highlights the action rather than labeling the person.
"You might want to reconsider your perspective."
Encourages reflection without being derogatory.
"There appears to be a misunderstanding."
Suggests a lack of comprehension rather than an intellectual deficit.
